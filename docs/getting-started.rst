Getting Started
===============

This guide will help you get started with the Sidechain plugin, from installation to your first recording.

Installation
------------

The Sidechain plugin is available in VST3 and AU (macOS) formats. Install the plugin to your system's standard plugin directory:

**macOS:**
- VST3: ``/Library/Audio/Plug-Ins/VST3/`` (system-wide) or ``~/Library/Audio/Plug-Ins/VST3/`` (user)
- AU: ``/Library/Audio/Plug-Ins/Components/`` (system-wide) or ``~/Library/Audio/Plug-Ins/Components/`` (user)

**Windows:**
- VST3: ``C:\Program Files\Common Files\VST3\``

**Linux:**
- VST3: ``/usr/lib/vst3/`` or ``~/.vst3/``

Loading the Plugin
------------------

1. Open your DAW (Ableton Live, Logic Pro, FL Studio, etc.)
2. Scan for new plugins (or restart your DAW)
3. Add Sidechain to a track as an effect plugin
4. The plugin window will open automatically

First Steps
-----------

1. **Create an Account**: Click "Sign Up" to create a new account, or "Log In" if you already have one
2. **Complete Your Profile**: Add a profile picture and bio to help others discover you
3. **Explore the Feed**: Browse posts from other producers in your feed
4. **Record Your First Clip**: Click the record button to capture audio from your DAW

Features
--------

* **Audio Capture**: Record audio directly from your DAW
* **Social Feed**: Share and discover music from other producers
* **Real-time Messaging**: Chat with other users
* **Stories**: Share short audio clips that expire after 24 hours
* **MIDI Capture**: Record and share MIDI patterns
* **Key Detection**: Automatically detect the musical key of your recordings
